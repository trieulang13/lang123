-: E-mail Editor Help :-

  • To anh Cc:
    To and Cc boxes allow you to enter the e-mail addresses of the recepients to whom to message has to be sent. Recipients will be able to see who else was sent the message by looking at the contents of the To and Cc boxes.
  • Subject:
    The Subject box allows to enter meaningful summary of the message.
  • Message:
    The MessageTextArea allows you to enter the text of your message.
